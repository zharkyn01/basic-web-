Welcome to the beach

The most popular beach of South Goa, Palolem is also one of the most picturesque beaches in the state. Enclosed by a thick forest of coconut palms, Palolem is popular amongst family vacationers as well as backpackers and young crowds. The beach is as perfect for a quiet laid-back holiday as for partying. The north side of the beach is where one can find family vacationers enjoying their quiet and relaxing holiday. In the centre and south of Palolem Beach, backpackers and young crowd can be seen hanging out at the beach shacks or enjoying watersports.

Palolem has a wide range of accommodation available in different categories. There beach huts for staying at Palolem Beach that range from budget to luxury categories. Hostels for backpackers are available and even heritage villa stay is also a possibility at Palolem Beach. Pack a picnic lunch and head to this relatively undiscovered beach to enjoy crystal-clear seawater, caramel-colored sand, and a grove of tropical coconut palm trees. he best time to Visit Palolem Beach is between October and March when the beach shacks are open and the weather is also quite pleasant.Palolem Beach is situated at a distance of about 10 minutes from its nearest railway station at Canacona.

Palolem Beach is situated at a distance of about 10 minutes from its nearest railway station at Canacona. Taxis and auto-rickshaws are readily available for Palolem from Canacona Railway Station. Bring enough water to last you through the day — there are no beach shacks to refuel at here, and the sun can be powerful.Palolem Beach doesn’t have much options for nightlife except for its unique Silent Noise Party at its southern end. Cafe Del Mar and Silver Star - Cocktails & Dreams are also where you can go for some good time at night. Sometimes, full moon parties are also hosted on Palolem Beach. If you want to enjoy dancing, visit Leopard Valley on the Palolem-Agonda Road which is South Goa's biggest outdoor dance club.

Boats can be hired from Palolem Beach for enjoying a great session of dolphin sighting. It is a great experience to sight a pod of dolphins or a single dolphin swimming along your boat. One can hire a boat to go on a fishing trip from Palolem Beach. An inexpensive trip along the backwater canals during the high tide can be enjoyed.Kayaking is possible along the backwater canals near Palolem Beach. It is in fact, a great way to explore the area.

During the low tide, one can even attempt to hike to the Butterfly Beach. It is the perfect place to catch the view of the beautiful sunset. A day trip from Palolem can be made to Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary, where the rich wildlife of Goa can be witnessed.Palolem has a strip of shops just behind the beach where one can buy a number of items including souvenirs and Bohomenian jewellery and clothing.

Learn making North Indian and Goan cuisine at Chef Rahul’s cooking classes at his kitchen on Palolem's main road, behind Cuba beach bungalow. One can avail a mixture of Ayurvedic massages, reflexology, Reiki and energy healing at Himalayan Therapeutic Studio. Learning/practising Yoga is also a possibility at Cozy Nook, Anand Yoga Village, Bhakti Kutir, Ruban Yoga and Ayurveda Village, Earth Yoga Village, and Dreamcatcher.
